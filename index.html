We like testing cars and commenting on how well they drive, as well as their exterior and interior designs.

Probably the ugliest car ever made, ever. The Nissan Juke was designed by Koji Wakabayashi, Takashi Utsunomiya, and Marten Wallgren back in 2009, and first debuted at the 2010 Geneva Motor Show in the March.

As far as the design goes, it is a very ugly car looking at it from the front and the back, with its bug-like appearance at the front, and its very bulbous rearend. We have not yet test-driven the car as of now, but we have plans to one day get hold of one and take it out for a spin, to see if, against all odds, the car's mechanics can make up for its shortfalls in the design department.
